About the Artist

Zehra Nur — Paper Artist

🌼 From paper flowers to stained glass, linocut, and water marbling — every workshop is a welcoming space to learn, make, and connect. Studio Artvika celebrates traditional craft techniques brought to life in a modern, cozy setting.

Based in Maura / Oslo, Norway, the studio offers intimate, hands-on workshops and events. Whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned crafter, you'll find creativity and community here.
